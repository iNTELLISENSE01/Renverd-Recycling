The problem

The Pioneers In The Recycling Service!

In 2018, Nigeria discharged approximately 200,000 tons of plastic waste into the ocean per year. Nigeria is ranked ninth for plastic pollution with an estimated 2.5 million tons of plastic waste generated annually and less than 12% is recycled.

In Nigeria the annual plastics production grew to over 530,000 tons in 2022. The increase in flood cases in Nigeria, have been attributed to obstructed gutters, drainages, and clogged water channel systems traceable to plastic bottles waste.

Renverd's solution

Our innovative solution system

Renverd Recycling is a social enterprise committed to tackling the plastic pollution crisis in Nigeria. Our innovative solution involves a weigh-and-pay system that incentivizes individuals to collect and return plastic waste.

By offering financial rewards for plastic, we empower communities to actively participate in waste management. This community-driven approach not only cleans up the environment but also creates job opportunities in waste collection and processing, stimulating local economic growth.
